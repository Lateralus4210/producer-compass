# Giordano Ruberto (Gio)

## Overview
Canadian music producer and multidisciplinary creative based in Thunder Bay, Ontario. Former MC who opened for national acts, now focused on production and building toward a long-term vision he calls "the Giodome" — a concept space blending music, sacred geometry, culinary arts, herbalism, and mindfulness. Warm, philosophical presence in the FP. Less interested in technical education content than in community and creative expression. One of the four members in The FP's first free FP+ cohort (March 2026).

## Background
- **Location:** Thunder Bay, Ontario, Canada
- **Status:** FP+ member (first cohort, March 2026)
- **DAW:** Ableton Live
- **Music background:** Former MC and rapper, 2012–2016. Opened for Tech N9ne, MGK, Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, Vinnie Paz, and other underground acts in Thunder Bay. Transitioned to production/behind-the-scenes work.
- **Relocation history:** Lived in Vancouver/BC 2016–2020, returned to Thunder Bay after relationship ended
- **Current work:** Tea shop employee; trained cook/culinary background
- **Additional interests:** Herbalism, sacred geometry, Tai Chi, yoga, mindfulness, poetry
- **Music releases:** Bandcamp and SoundCloud; no streaming platform presence yet as of Feb 2026
- **Style:** Bass music with hip-hop DNA; percussion-driven, sparse harmonic/melodic content
- **Goal:** Build the Giodome — a creative community/platform bridging music, sacred geometry, movement, and culinary arts; digital first, eventually physical (dojo/studio space)
- **Other communities:** Seed to Stage discord (active in Jamuary, Feedback February), briefly in Form and Flow (Steve's community, closed)

## Notes

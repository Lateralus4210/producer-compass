### ~Early–Mid March 2026 — Video Vault: "From Crummy to Yummy (Polishing Sounds)"
This Video Vault recording (Part I) was made by Zach and Skyler as a direct reaction to a sequence of Ansh's community posts. A technical companion (Part II) followed, covering FL Studio + Vital hands-on sound design. Documents his breakthrough arc:

- **Post 1:** Asked for advice — "I know what to make, but nothing I put into the DAW seems to have potential in my mind." - **Post 3 (double task):** Hit a wall on one track, switched gears, accidentally produced for 5 hours straight. Starting to feel more confident.
- Self-identified: works well in specific moments and is learning to recognize when those moments are vs. when to step back.
- Style direction crystallizing: **video game music / rhythmic art core** with embedded D&B stylistic concepts in the drop.
- Plans to polish and release his track.
- Zach and Skyler's core teaching: everything has potential; polishing is incremental (reverb, compression, leveling, sound swap, context of other instruments); don't scrap — come back later; "crummy" often means "bare" not "bad."
